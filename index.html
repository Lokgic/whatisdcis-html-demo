What is

DCIS?

DCIS is a condition of the milk ducts in the breast.

DCIS is not cancer.

However, it does increase your risk of getting breast cancer in the future.

DCIS ≠  Cancer

To reduce the risk of breast cancer in the future, there are several options to choose from.

Before we get there, it is very important to know that DCIS does not turn into cancer overnight

You have time to learn about the different options and take a decision that feels right for you!

There are two main options. You can choose to undergo surgery right away. Or you can choose to monitor your DCIS instead; this is called active surveillance.

If you choose to undergo surgery, you can choose between breast-conserving surgery (Lx), and removal of the entire breast (Mx)

In the case of breast breast conserving surgery (Lx), you can choose to undergo additional radiation treatment (Rx).

Hover on any of the options to learn more about the procedure.

Each of these options has its pros and cons.

Ultimately you have to take a decision that feels right for you you.
